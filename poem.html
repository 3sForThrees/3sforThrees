A Thousand Words, One Name

Through my darkest times you are my light,
My guiding star, my heart's delight.
The great mountains and sceneries,
All pale in comparison to your beauty.

Your laughter is my favorite song,
and your smile, I remember all day long.
Such things I am willing to learn and do,
for it is all for you.

Who do I speak of, you may ask?
A name that carries a thousand words unmask.
someone whose name rhymes with matcha,
it is no other than you, Lara.
